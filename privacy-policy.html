PRIVACY POLICY

Information We Collect

• Discord User Information:  We collect your Discord User ID, and may collect your Discord username. This information is necessary for the Bot to function within the Discord platform and identify you.
‍• Server Information: We collect the Discord Server ID where the Bot is used. This is necessary for the Bot to operate within specific servers. We may also collect the Server Name.
• Message Content:  The Bot logs message content, including text messages, embeds, and attachments (images). This is a core feature of the Bot as it is required to provide accurate logs for server administrators. We may also log message timestamps and the channel ID where the message was sent.
‍• Image Content: The Bot logs image content sent in Discord channels. This is to allow server administrators to have access to data sent in their server. We may also log image timestamps and the channel ID where the image was sent.
• Configuration Data: Server administrators may configure certain settings for the Bot. We store this configuration data, which may include channel whitelists/blacklists, role permissions, and other customized settings.
• Error Logs:  We collect error logs to identify and fix issues with the Bot. These logs may contain information about the error, the timestamp, and the context in which the error occurred. These logs do not include message content unless the error is directly related to the processing of a specific message.

How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:
‍• Bot Functionality: To provide the core features of the Bot, such as logging and statistics.
‍• Bot Improvement: To analyze usage patterns and identify areas for improvement in the Bot's functionality and performance.
‍• Moderation: The logged data is used to enforce server rules and maintain a safe environment.
• Support: To provide support to users and server administrators.
• Legal Compliance: To comply with applicable laws and regulations.

How We Share Your Information

We will not share your information with third parties except in the following circumstances:
• Legal Requirements: We may disclose your information if required by law, court order, or government request.
• Service Providers: We may share your information with trusted service providers who assist us in operating the Bot (e.g., hosting providers). These service providers are contractually obligated to protect your information.
• Server Administrators: Server administrators have access to the data collected by the Bot within their respective servers. This includes message logs, image logs, and command usage. We are not responsible for how server administrators use this data.
• With Your Consent: We may share your information with third parties with your explicit consent.

Data Retention

We retain your information forever. You can request deletion of your data by contacting us at coalrlp81@gmail.com. However, please note that server administrators may have their own data retention policies for the data they access through the Bot.

Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, use, or disclosure. However, no method of transmission over the internet, or method of electronic storage, is 100% secure.

Children's Privacy

Our Service is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you become aware that a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, please contact us.
